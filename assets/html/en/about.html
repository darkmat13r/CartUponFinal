Returns, Refunds & Privacy Policy

What is CartUpon products Warranties?

In general, our products will have the original distributors warranty and full instructions will be given for every deal. CartUpon.com does not offer warranties except for dead on arrival items where we are committed to our full refund policy. Please read the warranty information on each Deal.


What is your Return & Refund policy?

CartUpon offers full refund on defective products and electronics that are Dead on Arrival (DoA). Please contact our customer service department to register your claim within 3 business days of receiving your product and we will help with your claim. email us on info#cartupon.com 


What is your Privacy Policy? Do you save my credit card information?

CartUpon.Com offers a clear certified privacy policy. CartUpon does not save any of your private financial information and will never use your personal data beyond what is explained in the privacy policy. Our primary purpose in collecting personal information is to provide you with a safe, smooth, efficient, and customized experience while shopping on CartUpon.Com
